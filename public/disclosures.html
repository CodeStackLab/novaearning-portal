Risk disclosure

Returns are not guaranteed. Digital-asset transfers and investment-style products can involve substantial risk, including loss, delays, price changes and operational failure. Only use funds you can afford to lose.

Earnings and plan information

Displayed projections are estimates based on the stated plan rate and duration. They are not promises of future performance. Your dashboard transaction record and applicable written plan terms determine account activity.

Fees and withdrawals

Fees and minimum withdrawal requirements must be shown before submission. Blockchain transactions may be irreversible. Verify wallet network and destination details carefully.

Account security

Keep credentials and verification codes private. Nova support should never request your password. Report unfamiliar sign-ins or transactions immediately through the support center.

Privacy

Account, device, transaction and support information may be processed to operate the service, prevent fraud, provide support and meet applicable legal obligations. Do not upload unnecessary sensitive personal data.
