How can we help you?

Find answers to our most frequently asked questions.

How do I make a deposit?

You can make a deposit by navigating to the Deposit page in your dashboard. Select one of the preset amounts or click "Custom Amount" to enter your desired investment. Send the exact USDT (TRC20) to the displayed wallet address, and don't forget to upload a screenshot of your transaction receipt for quick verification.

When do I get my investment returns?

Once your deposit is approved by our team, your investment cycle begins automatically. Our automated system calculates your 2.5% daily profit, and exactly 24 hours after approval, your principal amount plus the accrued profit will be credited directly to your account balance.

How does the referral program work?

You earn a generous 10% commission on every approved deposit made by your referrals! Furthermore, as they earn daily profits, you also earn a 10% commission on their earnings. Simply share your unique referral link found in the Referrals section of your dashboard.

How do I request a withdrawal?

Go to the Withdraw page in your dashboard. Ensure your TRON wallet address is correctly set up in your Profile. Enter the amount you wish to withdraw, and submit your request. Our team processes withdrawals swiftly once verified.
